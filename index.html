Branches of Mathematics
by Wikipedia

Using the links of Wikipedia webpage about mathematics, this project classifies various branches of mathematics.

  • Classifying Mathematics

    The branches of a field of study in these days are so diverse, specified and inter-related with many area of studies. So, it is hard to determine if a topic from a discipline belongs to any of its sub field. Wikipedia is the biggest online encyclopedia which contains information about almost anything that can be documented with expertsâ€™ level of depth. Moreover, it consists of hyperlinked web pages, any two topics closely related to each other have a link from one to another of vice versa. Web pages of Wikipedia form a massive network itself. So, if you can get an information of the connections between web pages, you may analyze the topics in Wikipedia using techniques of classifying communities in networks. In this project, using links between popular mathematics topics in Wikipedia pages, I classify the sub fields of mathematics by their topics.


    GitHub: https://github.com/euijin-hong/fields_of_mathematics
    Dataset: https://archive.ics.uci.edu/dataset/605/wikipedia+math+essentials
    Algorithm: Graph Partition, BigCLAM
    Language: Python
    Packages: json, numpy, matplotlib, networkx
